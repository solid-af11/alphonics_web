About

The Sound
of Alphonics

Alphonics is more than a band — it's a frequency. Born in Delhi's underground scene, five individuals bound by a shared obsession: music that hits the body before it reaches the mind.

From delicate acoustic fingerpicking to thunderous electric riffs, from hushed harmonies to raw percussive force — our sound refuses category. We play where genres dissolve.
